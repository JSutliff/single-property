This incomparable custom built residence has been retained by the original owners and is now offered as a once in a lifetime legacy purchase. Providing the most enviable location within the Belleairs, and the most comprehensive Intracoastal waterway views over Clearwater Harbor, the Sand Key skyline, the Sand Key Bridge, South & North Clearwater Beach, the new Clearwater Memorial, and the vast northwest sweep to Dunedin and beyond, the vistas are not to be duplicated. An exceptional free flowing floor plan allows surreal water views from every vantage point, soaring ceilings allow an extraordinary architectural scale and scope seldom found. A superb integration of living and family rooms alongside an open kitchen lends seamlessly to a loggia terrace capturing 270 degree panoramas that are spectacular by day, and compelling by night. Four en suite bedrooms face Clearwater Harbor directly, (3) of which also access terraces, an intended upstairs media room may serve as additional recreational space, a dedicated play room, or a second office/den. Exquisitely appointed with stone and hardwood flooring, custom cabinetry, superior appliances inclusive of Sub zero, Thermador and Asko, marble baths, bespoke millwork and timeless lighting, the interiors are quite fabulous. An incredible 10 car garage provides phenomenal storage space beyond measure, the dock accommodates a 42 foot vessel.
